The Art Story

A journey between shadow and light

All My Stories

Since childhood, I have looked for beauty where others saw chaos. Each canvas is an attempt to catch a moment of truth—fragile, imperfect, alive.

I paint to understand. To give form to what moves through us: doubt, impulse, fear, love. When I work, I quiet the world. Colors speak for me.

“Art does not reproduce the visible; it makes visible.”

Evolving with every canvas

Years have led me through many materials and gestures: oil, acrylic, collage, patient glazes and sudden bursts. I don’t have a style; I have a quest. Each series is a step, each failure a revelation.

“Every brushstroke is a choice, and every choice a trace of oneself.”

Evolving with every canvas

I often return to a piece months later. Nothing is fixed: the light changes, and so do I. The canvas becomes a silent journal where I read my own transformations.

Art as an inner mirror

My art grows from a love of detail, patience, and the desire to make things more beautiful than they believe themselves to be. I move gently, even in the heart of disorder.
